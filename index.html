From Chaos to Components: My Denver Love Affair with Storybook

When I joined the company, I noticed we kept dragging designs between projects. I explained Storybook’s tech and how it saves time and money. The manager backed me up—and just like that, I carried the whole approval process. We built on Material UI, and our design team added beautiful custom touches. Everyone sighed with relief—now we could finally focus on layout and middleware.

Keeping the Flow as One: My Joy with the Booking App

We started building a booking app with only a basic flow for the customer—it was clear, and I truly enjoyed it. Suddenly, the vendor and supervisor roles were added. That’s when I realized how powerful it is to understand the full project flow. I had a complete view of the system, and the feeling of control was truly empowering. With me, you go to Denver.
